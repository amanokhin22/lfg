Who we are

Lightfury Games is built by gamers, for gamers. Our journey started with one mission: to create groundbreaking, immersive, and competitive gaming experiences that inspire and connect players worldwide. We’re a team of industry veterans from Ubisoft, EA, Tencent, and Improbable—each bringing years of expertise in developing AAA titles. Our shared passion fuels our drive to push boundaries, evolve with the industry, and craft games that redefine what’s possible.

Our Vision

To pioneer a new era of gaming that redefines the future for everyone, everywhere.

Mission

Create groundbreaking gaming experiences that inspire, entertain, and connect players globally.

Values We Live & Breathe

Innovation – We push the limits of creativity and tech, bringing new gameplay mechanics and engaging narratives.

Inclusivity

We believe gaming is for everyone. Different perspectives make for better stories.

Excellence

Every detail matters. Our focus is on delivering top-tier experiences.

Passion

Gaming isn’t just our job—it’s what drives us to create.

Integrity

Transparency, honesty, and respect shape everything we do.
