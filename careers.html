Life at
LightFury

At Lightfury Games, we’re here to redefine the future of gaming—building next-gen experiences that push the boundaries of play. We’re a team of passionate creators, engineers, and storytellers working together to craft games that entertain, inspire, and connect players worldwide.

Here, you’re not just part of a company—you’re part of a global gaming movement. Whether it’s designing immersive worlds, fine-tuning gameplay physics, or developing cutting-edge AI, every role contributes to something bigger. At Lightfury, your work matters.

Meet your
future
team

Gaming is a team sport, and at Lightfury Games, we’ve assembled some of the best players in the industry. Our leadership and development teams come from Ubisoft, EA, Rockstar Games, Wargaming, and more—bringing their expertise from titles like Assassin’s Creed, GTA V, Sea of Thieves, and Batman: Arkham Origins.
Join us, and work alongside artists, designers, engineers, and producers who share your passion for innovation, storytelling, and game design.

Where you can find us

We’re headquartered in India and the UK, but our reach is truly global. With team members from Ukraine, Spain, and beyond, we thrive on diverse perspectives and international collaboration.

No matter where you are, if you’re passionate about AAA game development, there’s a place for you at Lightfury.
